# P30-H3 Action-Specific Span-Cost Accounting

- Schema: `p30-h3-action-span-cost-report-v1`
- This report is generated by `eval/p30_admission_model_v3.py` as a dedicated score-phase accounting artifact and written to `artifacts/p30_admission_v3/p30_h3_span_cost_report.json`.
- Status: `score_phase_only_accounting=true`, `diagnostic_only=true`, `promotion_ready=false`, `default_should_change=false`.

## Purpose

P30-H3 is **accounting-only** and **not a new admission route or policy**. It derives deterministic action-specific span-cost accounting from existing policies (`candidate_baseline`, `llm_span_narrow`, `llm_filter`, `llm_abstain_filter`, `bucket_routed_v0`, `admission_v3`, `admission_v3_h1`, `admission_v3_h2`) without changing routes, EvidenceCore semantics, or default strategies.

For every policy, H3 reports per-action aggregates such as:

- `selected_count`, `selected_rate`
- `action_added_gold_span`, `action_added_false_span`
- `false_per_gold`, `gold_per_false`
- `net_span_value_1x`, `net_span_value_2x`
- `delta_added_gold_span_vs_baseline`, `delta_added_false_span_vs_baseline`
- `mean_delta_span_f0_5_vs_baseline`, `mean_delta_primary_false_positive_rate_vs_baseline`
- `gold_kill_rate`, `false_reduction_rate`
- `budget_violated`, `budget_violation_reason`

Policy-level summaries include:

- `primary_false_span_cost`, `non_primary_false_span_cost`, `unclassified_false_span_cost`
- `budget_violation_count`, `budget_violation_rate`, `budget_violation_reasons`
- `worst_actions_by_false_cost`
- `worst_actions_by_gold_kill`

## Budget policy (accounting-only)

- Primary-admit actions (`admit_symbol_regex_union`, `admit_rrf_primary`, `admit_llm_span_narrow`): `added_false_span <= added_gold_span`.
- Non-primary actions (`abstain`, `apply_llm_filter`, `supporting_only`, `weak_candidate_only`): `added_false_span == 0`.
- Unclassified baseline strategy actions (`candidate_baseline`, `llm_span_narrow`, `llm_filter`, `llm_abstain_filter`): expected net-neutral, `added_false_span <= added_gold_span`.

Budget violations are diagnostic flags used to identify which actions still carry span-level false cost. They are **not** a production cost constraint.

## Safety notes

- P30-H3 is score-phase accounting over fixed existing policies; it does not route or admit tasks.
- No remote model calls are made during H3 accounting.
- No raw queries, snippets, prompts, responses, gold spans, private labels, provider keys, or per-task records are emitted.
- `promotion_ready=false`, `default_should_change=false`, `evidencecore_semantics_changed=false`, `candidate_not_fact=true`, `external_calls=0`.
